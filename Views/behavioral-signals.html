Capabilities

Claim-Level Extraction
Tags are not words — they are claims. "Input cost increase," "pricing resistance," "demand softening." Each with polarity, certainty, scope, and entity linkage.
Management Language Patterns
Detection of hedging, non-committal guidance, cost-focus shifts, and macro-blame patterns. These correlate strongly with forward returns and have high informational content.
Narrative Correlation Analysis
Shared narrative identification across entities. Detects when "AI capex" or "regulatory risk" narratives cluster stocks, distinguishing signal from noise.
Sentiment Divergence Detection
When sentiment disagrees with fundamentals or price action, that divergence is itself a tradable signal. Tracked and scored as a z-score anomaly.
Source Credibility Scoring
Not all sources are equal. Institutional research carries more weight than social posts. Source-specific hit rates calibrated against historical price responses.
Temporal Sentiment Decay
How quickly does sentiment impact fade? Half-life estimation per signal type and entity, critical for position timing and sizing.
