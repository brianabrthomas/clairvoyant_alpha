Fundamental Valuation

DCF modeling with footnote-level granularity, dynamically selected discount rates conditional on macro regime, comparable company analysis, and earnings quality assessment. This is the valuation prior — VDCFt — the anchor from which all other signals are measured.

Intrinsic Value Anchor VDCFt = Σn=1..N FCFn / (1 + rregime)n + TV / (1 + rregime)N

Capabilities

Footnote-Level DCF
Full financial statement decomposition with line-item granularity. Revenue segments, operating leverage, capex cycles, working capital dynamics, and terminal value sensitivity.
Regime-Conditional Discount Rates
Interest rates dynamically selected based on current macro regime state. Risk premia adjust for inflation expectations, credit conditions, and policy stance.
Comparable Analysis
Peer-relative valuation using EV/EBITDA, P/E, P/FCF across sector cohorts. Multiples decomposed by growth, margin, and capital intensity.
Earnings Quality Scoring
Accrual models, cash conversion tracking, revenue recognition flags, and footnote-derived risk indicators for each reporting period.
Scenario Modeling
Bull / base / bear intrinsic values with mechanically defined assumptions per regime. Posterior probability weighting across scenarios.
Valuation Gap Analysis
Market price vs. intrinsic value z-score tracking. Historical percentile ranking of current mispricing relative to own history and sector peers.

Statistical Artifacts (Precomputed)

Valuation Distributions
Posterior distributions of intrinsic value under each scenario, stored as compressed samples with HDI intervals and probability of undervaluation.
Factor Decomposition
Rolling and regime-conditional betas to Fama-French factors, momentum, liquidity, and volatility. Shapley attribution of valuation gap drivers.
Earnings Persistence Models
MCMC-derived posteriors for accrual quality, revenue durability, and margin sustainability metrics per reporting entity.
