Regime & Context

Sector stability, macro sensitivity, and cross-sector dynamics provide the contextual fabric that determines how strongly each information signal transmits into price. The regime response kernel E[Rk,t | Mt] answers: "In THIS regime, does this type of information matter?"

Capabilities

Sector Correlation Dynamics
Rolling inter-sector and intra-sector correlation tracking. Correlation spikes detected as regime change signals. Cross-asset contagion pathways mapped in graph view.
Macro Sensitivity Scoring
Per-asset beta to each macro state. Which stocks are most sensitive to rate changes, credit conditions, or growth slowdowns? Regime-conditional sensitivity maps.
Structural Break Detection
CUSUM, Bai-Perron, and Bayesian change-point tests identify when relationships shift permanently vs. temporarily. Critical for distinguishing cyclical from secular changes.
Sector Rotation Models
Which sectors lead and lag across regime transitions? Historical rotation patterns mapped to transition probabilities for anticipatory positioning.
Nested Regime Hierarchies
Matrices inside matrices: macro regime → sector sub-regime → company micro-state. Hierarchical Markov models capture multi-scale dynamics.
Historical Analog Search
Semantic similarity search across historical regime states. "When did the current environment last look like this?" with outcome distributions from matched periods.
