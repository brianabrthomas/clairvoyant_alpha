Capabilities

Volatility Surface Modeling
Full IV surface reconstruction across strikes and expiries. SVI parameterization for arbitrage-free interpolation. Real-time surface updates with delta-hedged P&L tracking.
Skew Analysis
25-delta risk reversal, butterfly spreads, and skew percentile scoring. Detects asymmetric fear/greed pricing across the strike space.
IV Term Structure
Contango/backwardation detection across expiry tenors. Event-driven vol bumps isolated from baseline term structure. Variance swap fair value computation.
Realized vs Implied Divergence
Volatility risk premium (VRP) tracking. Historical percentile ranking of IV-RV spread. Regime-conditional VRP behavior for optimal vol selling/buying timing.
Gamma & Flow Analysis
Dealer gamma exposure estimation, max pain levels, and open interest concentration. Identifies mechanical hedging flows that amplify or dampen moves.
Cross-Asset Vol Signals
VIX term structure, rate vol (MOVE), credit vol, and FX vol correlation analysis. Identifies regime shifts via cross-asset volatility divergence patterns.
